About Damodar Traders

Leading the industry in quality pipe fittings and industrial solutions since 2011

Our Story

Established in 2011, Damodar Traders began as a small family-owned business with a vision to provide high-quality industrial products to the local market. Under the insightful guidance of our founder, Damodar Prasad Sharma, we have grown into a trusted name in the industry.

Starting with a modest inventory of basic pipe fittings, we have expanded our product range to include a comprehensive selection of pipes, valves, fittings, and other industrial components. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has been the driving force behind our growth.

Over the years, we have served numerous clients across various industries, from small workshops to large industrial complexes, building lasting relationships based on trust, reliability, and exceptional service.

Our Mission

To provide the highest quality industrial products and solutions while maintaining competitive prices and exceptional customer service. We strive to be the most reliable partner for all your industrial needs.

Our Vision

To become the leading supplier of industrial products in the region, recognized for our quality, reliability, and innovative solutions that help our customers succeed in their businesses.
